Symptoms from following areas -

  1. Problems with socialization (2 or more)
    • Difficulty using non speech interaction(difficulty in eye contact), difficulty in gestures, tone of voice
    • peer relationships(cant make friends of same age)
    • lack of seeking enjoyment, achievements etc
    • lack of social response, lack of emotional response(if someone near gets hurt they may not respond or ask if they're okay).
  2. Problems with communication (1 or more)
    • delay or lack language, or may not use proper pronouns(grammar).
    • if they have proper language, difficulties starting a conversation or not interested in bringing topics in conversation, avoid conversation, getting frustated at instructions.
    • repetitive use of language (may frequently speak words they recall from a movie they saw or a song)
    • trouble imitating others.
  3. Repititive or inflexible behavior
    • Obsessed with limited interests
    • Stimming - Self Stimulatory Behaviors (SSBs) (Children with autism develop self rewarding behaviors. It is important for trainers/care-takers to sense these behaviors). Repititive Body movements - repititive jumping, flapping hands, spinning in circles.
      • Visual - eye gazing - gazing at other objects.
      • Auditory - make noises themselves in order to hear them.
      • Oral - teeth grinding, playing with saliva.
      • Smell - smelling their hands or certain objects.

Learning Apps

  • Therapy apps following DTT - Discrete Trial Training and PRT - Pivotal Response Treatment methods of ABA Training.
  • These apps will be in the form of entertaining learning games.
  • Proved to be helpful as part of research done under ABA. View ABA Case Study to learn about ABA - PRT and DTT.
  • Can track Progress in various domains of growth and thus provide adaptability.

Use Cases -

  • Select a Game to play.
  • Suggest Games.

Learning on

  • Colours
  • Shapes
  • Animals
  • Body Parts
  • Emotions (Emoticons)
  • Gestures
  • Vegetables/Fruits
  • Numbers
  • Picture Matching
  • Sound Games

Games can be categorised into various abilities - Mathematical, Cognition, Emotion etc. Progress in each game/ability can be recorded. Correspondingly, Recommendations can be made.
